    üìç Laundry Groups

    The Laundry Group level is the main level where you can edit all relevant settings for your users for washing and drying.

    ‚ûï Create Laundry Groups

    The most important first step at Smart Schulthess is creating the Laundry Group. Click on ‚ÄúCREATE NEW - "

    • Choose a name, e.g. the name of your Laundry or your property.
    • Enter the external payout reference, email address, IBAN number and billing address for the monthly credit.

    Note

    You can leave the coordinates blank ‚Äì these are especially important for public laundries.

    • Select your currency and payment method.The default is prepaid. This enables your users to pay via a prepaid credit. Direct Pay is also possible, but this is associated with higher fees. You can then activate the desired functions.

    Note

    Note that these incur higher licence fees. Please contact your Schulthess advisor for the current licence prices.

    • Finally, you need to select service package plan.
